    SUPERCAR ALL THE WORLD

    Ferrari F80
    launched - hybrid supercar priced at 3.9 million USD

    More about Ferrari F80

    This recent model, unveiled as the successor to the LaFerrari, boasts a hybrid V6 engine delivering a combined 1,200 horsepower. It emphasizes lightweight design through the use of carbon fiber and aerodynamics derived from Formula 1 technology. The F80 introduces advanced active aerodynamics, including an active rear wing and diffuser. Limited to 799 units, the F80 is Ferrari's most powerful road car to date

    LaFerrari
    launched - hybrid supercar priced at 3.9 million USD

    More about LaFerrari

    TThis flagship hypercar, introduced in 2013, features a hybrid powertrain combining a V12 engine with an electric motor, producing over 950 horsepower. The LaFerrari was designed with advanced aerodynamics and has a top speed exceeding 350 km/h. Only 499 units were produced, with an additional open-top version called the LaFerrari Aperta released later.

    Ferrari KC23
    launched - hybrid supercar priced at 3.9 million USD

    More about Ferrari KC23

    The KC23 was unveiled at the 2023 Goodwood Festival of Speed and later exhibited at Ferrari's museum in Maranello. It draws inspiration from the 499P Hypercar and emphasizes minimalism with its stripped-back interior, aligning more closely with Ferrari's endurance racing philosophy than traditional road cars. While technically not a mainstream production model, it stands as a pinnacle of Ferrari's bespoke craftsmanship, made for a visionary collector and occasionally showcased to the public at special events.
